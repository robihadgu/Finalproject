How Deepfakes Are Made

Step 1 ¬∑ Collect real data

The system needs lots of examples of a person‚Äôs face and voice. It learns how they look from different angles and how they sound when they speak.

Step 2 ¬∑ Train the AI model

A Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) has two parts: one creates fake faces, another checks if they look real. They train together until the fake images are hard to detect.

Step 3 ¬∑ Produce the deepfake

The trained model overlays the learned face or voice onto new footage. The final video looks like normal camera footage, even though it is synthetic.

Ethics: How We Judge Deepfakes

Lens 1 ¬∑ Utilitarianism

Question: Overall, more good or more harm?

  • Good: film effects, education, disability support.
  • Harms: misinformation, fraud, harassment, and loss of trust.

Our analysis: today, the total harm is larger than the total benefit, unless strong controls are added.
